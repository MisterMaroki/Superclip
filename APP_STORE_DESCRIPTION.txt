Superclip

A clipboard manager for macOS that keeps track of what you copy and makes it easy to access and edit your clipboard history.

Clipboard Management
Access your clipboard history from anywhere with a keyboard shortcut. Browse through previously copied items including text, images, files, and URLs. Items are automatically saved as you copy them.

Quick Editing
Edit clipboard items before pasting. Select any item from your history, make changes, and paste the edited version. Supports both plain text and rich text editing.

Text Sniper
Extract text from images or videos displayed on your screen. Select a region and the app uses OCR to recognize and copy the text. Works with any visible content including video frames, screenshots, or images in other applications.

Pinboards
Create pinboards to store frequently used items. Organize into named collections with color coding. Pinboards persist across sessions, so you can keep reusable text, code, or other content always accessible.

Snippets
Define trigger shortcuts that expand into full text anywhere on your Mac. Type ;;email and it becomes your email address. Create as many snippets as you need, toggle them on or off, and they work in every app.

Quick Actions
Superclip recognizes what you copy. Colors are shown with a swatch and can be converted between hex, RGB, and HSL. JSON can be pretty printed or minified. Emails, phone numbers, and file paths get contextual actions too.

Fuzzy Search
Start typing to search your history. Results are ranked by relevance, matching across content, source app, and file names.

Paste Stack
Sequential pasting mode for copying multiple items in a row. Copy several items, then paste them one after another. The app automatically advances to the next item after each paste.
